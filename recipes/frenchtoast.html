French Toast

This is an italian dish of cheese and pasta. If you hate Mondays and have an owner names Jon you will probably love this dish

Ingreident:

  • Cinnamon
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons Sugar

Preparation:

  1. Combine all items
  2. Dip Bread in Mixture
  3. Fry on each side until golden brown
